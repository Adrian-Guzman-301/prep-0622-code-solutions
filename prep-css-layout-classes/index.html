Indiana Jones

The Last Crusade

Somehow Indiana Jones has forgotten all of the lessons of its forebears, both film and character. It's one thing to make each film similar, it's another to give them amnesia.

Raiders of the Lost Ark

The picture is offering you a pact: you agree to be a kid again, in return for which Raiders will give you old-time movie thrills expressed in slick modern cinematic terms. No, thanks.

Temple of Doom

The film as a whole feels like an insignificant episode in Indy's life, instead of an influential chapter in the evolution of the action genre.
